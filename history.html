History of Hallownest


Once a prosperous kingdom, Hallownest has been reduced to ruins, with the majority of its population dead, missing, or overcome by the Infection. At the surface stands the small village of Dirtmouth, whence the last of the civilised bugs were eventually drawn into the depths of the kingdom. Elderbug tells the tragic tale of the town and of the many adventurers before the player.
